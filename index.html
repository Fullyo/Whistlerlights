Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered.

We recommend booking as early as possible — ideally in September or October — to ensure your preferred installation date. Our schedule for October-December fills up fast! Contact us today to secure your spot.

While our name is Whistler Lights, we are proud to serve the entire Sea-to-Sky corridor and Metro Vancouver, including Squamish, Pemberton, and North Vancouver. We bring our premium lighting services to homes and businesses throughout the region.

We exclusively use commercial-grade, non-consumer products because they are built to last for years, not just a single season. They are more durable, brighter, and far more reliable than store-bought lights. This commitment to quality ensures a stunning, professional display and allows us to stand behind our workmanship and product warranties.

Each installation is unique, so we provide a custom estimate based on photos or an on-site visit. Our minimum charge for a small project is $600 + tax, which includes installation, maintenance, and removal. Product costs are additional and vary depending on the scope of the project. We always provide a detailed estimate before beginning any work.

Our service includes season-long maintenance. If a bulb burns out or a strand comes loose, simply give us a call, and we will come fix it at no charge. It's all part of our commitment to a hassle-free experience.

While our roofline lights are durable, they are designed for seasonal use (up to 7 months). Prolonged exposure to the elements can cause fading and wear. Leaving lights up for more than 7 months will void our workmanship and product warranty.

Our schedule fills up very quickly, especially from October onwards. To ensure we can get your lights up in time for the holidays, we highly recommend booking your consultation and securing your installation date as early as possible. Contact us today to reserve your spot!
